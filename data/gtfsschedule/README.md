# GTFS Schedule dataset for Metropolitan Trains

From Transport Victoria's [OpenData portal](https://opendata.transport.vic.gov.au/dataset/gtfs-schedule). A subset of the static dataset containing 30 days of data from export date.

Includes things like timetable, routes and stations with keys/IDs to match with other APIs like the GTFS Realtime feeds.

1 (Regional Train)
2 (Metropolitan Train)
3 (Metropolitan Tram)
4 Myki Bus (Metro Bus and Regional Town Bus)
5 (Regional Coach)
6 (Regional Bus)
10 (Interstate)
11 (SkyBus)

Dataset dated 14/11/2025.
